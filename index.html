Kinematics is conveniently located in the Melbourne suburb of Richmond, where we offer the highest quality of holistic health services.

Our clinicians specialise in the assessment of functional and sport specific biomechanics (movements) of the whole body in order to identify areas of imbalance. If not addressed, these imbalances and poor mechanics can often lead to secondary compensations in the body and cascade on to cause other injuries.

We complement our musculoskeletal services with nutrition in order to improve your health from a well-rounded perspective. Through individualised dietary counselling, our dietitian will guide you towards establishing the optimal diet for your specific goals, sporting performance, or medical condition. Optimising your nutrition is essential to feeling and performing at your best.

This holistic, full-body approach provides an effective treatment plan for not only the presenting issue but also the underlying primary cause. Therefore reducing the risk of persistence and re-occurrence of the injury or condition.

Whether your goal is to become pain free, increase sporting fitness and performance, or improve general mobility and wellbeing, the team at Kinematics can help.
